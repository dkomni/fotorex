# Fotorex — Client Guide

This is your guide for uploading and managing photos and videos in your
gallery. You do not need any technical knowledge — everything happens in your
web browser.

## What you have

- **Public gallery:** `https://fotorex.pages.dev`
  Anyone with this link can browse albums and view your photos & videos.
- **Admin page:** `https://fotorex.pages.dev/admin.html`
  Only you can use this. You'll need the password you were given.

> Bookmark both pages.

---

## Signing in

1. Open the admin page (`/admin.html`).
2. Type your password and press **Enter**.
3. You'll see two tabs at the top: **Upload** and **Manage**.

The password is remembered until you close your browser window.

---

## Uploading photos & videos

1. Open the **Upload** tab.
2. **Pick an album** from the dropdown — or click **+ New album** and type a
   name (e.g. *summer-2025*).
3. **Drag your files** onto the dashed box, or click the box and choose them.
   You can drop dozens of files at once.
4. Each file shows a progress bar. When it says **Done**, that file is live in
   the gallery immediately.

### What works
- Photos: JPG, PNG, HEIC, WEBP, etc.
- Videos: MP4, MOV, WEBM.

### Tips
- **Album names** should be short, no spaces — use dashes (e.g. `wedding-2025`).
- **Large videos** (over ~100 MB) are also supported; they may take longer to
  upload but the page handles them automatically.
- You can keep working on other tabs/browser tabs while files are uploading —
  just don't close the admin tab until everything says **Done**.

---

## Deleting photos, videos or whole albums

1. Open the **Manage** tab.
2. Click any album to expand it. You'll see thumbnails of every item.
3. To **delete one item**: click the small **×** in the top-right corner of
   the thumbnail. Confirm the prompt.
4. To **delete the entire album**: click the red **Delete album** button next
   to the album name. Confirm the prompt.

> ⚠️ Deletion is permanent. There is no undo. Be sure before you confirm.

---

## Signing out

Click **Sign out** at the top-right. You'll need the password again next time.

---

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Try |
| ------- | --- |
| "Incorrect password" when you know it's right | Check Caps Lock, then try again. |
| Upload stuck on "Uploading…" for a long time | Large videos can take a while. If it shows **Error**, try again. |
| A photo doesn't appear in the gallery | Refresh the public gallery page (Ctrl+F5). |
| Wrong file accidentally uploaded | Go to **Manage → expand album → click × on it**. |

If something else goes wrong, contact your developer.
